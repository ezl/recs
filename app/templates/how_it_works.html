How Recs Works

Get personalized travel recommendations from friends who know your destination best.

The Recommendation Process

  1. 1

    Create a Trip

    Enter your destination and trip details. We'll create a unique link that you can share with friends.

  2. 2

    Share with Friends

    Send your unique link to friends who know your destination well. They can add their favorite spots and insider tips.

  3. 3

    Collect Recommendations

    Friends add their favorite places, activities, or experiences in your destination. They can include links to websites, reviews, or booking pages.

  4. 4

    Explore Your Personalized Guide

    All recommendations are collected on your trip page, creating a personalized travel guide curated by people you trust.

Adding Recommendations

When adding recommendations, you can:

  • Share favorite spots: Add your favorite places, activities, or experiences
  • Include details: Add tips, personal experiences, and specific recommendations
  • Add links: Include links to websites, reviews, or booking pages
  • Use voice input: Record your recommendations with your voice for a faster experience
